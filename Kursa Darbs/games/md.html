A spin-off of a popular sandbox title, Dungeons introduces a completely new approach to Minecraft gameplay. Instead of focusing on creative process. MS emphasizes dungeon-crawling action. The game features traditional RPG mechanics, such as character classes, progression through leveling, looting enemies for items and more.
